The chart presents the percentage of adult drinkers (15 years and older) who have engaged in heavy episodic drinking within the past 30 days in the year 2016 for three different countries. Heavy episodic drinking is defined here as consuming at least 60 grams of pure alcohol on one occasion. Ukraine has the highest proportion with 28.3% of its adult drinkers participating in such heavy drinking episodes. Sudan follows with 18.1% of drinkers reported to have had a heavy drinking session. Iran has the lowest percentage among the three, with only 4.1% of its adult drinkers consuming a significant amount of alcohol in a single session within that timeframe. These figures provide insight into the prevalence of heavy alcohol use among drinkers in different societal contexts.
