The provided chart details the proportion of clinical trials that reported results within a year on the European Clinical Trials Register (EU-CTR) by different countries for the year 2021. The United Kingdom stands out with the highest compliance rate at 93.6%, which is significantly above all other listed countries. Belgium follows as the second-best with a compliance rate of 68.6%. On the opposite end, France shows the lowest compliance with only 3.9% of its clinical trials reporting results within the designated period. The data reveals a broad disparity in reporting practices across Europe, highlighting the variation in adherence to reporting requirements. The mentioned countries, except the UK, have sizable room for improvement to meet the expected standard of timely clinical trial result reporting.
