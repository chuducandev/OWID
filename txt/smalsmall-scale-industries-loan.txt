The chart illustrates the proportion of small-scale industries with a loan or line of credit across different regions in 2023. Oceania leads with nearly 45% of its small-scale industries having access to financing, closely followed by Latin America and the Caribbean at 44.2%. Europe also shows a significant proportion with 41.2%. All these regions outpace the global average, which is 30.3%. Asia's small-scale industries with access to credit stands at 23.6%, setting it below the world average. Africa, as a whole, shows lower access to credit at 15.3%. Northern Africa has the lowest proportion among the regions listed, with just 13.4% of small-scale industries holding a loan or line of credit, indicating potential challenges in credit access for small businesses in that region.
